How to Play Dice game.

1. This is a game between player 1 and player 2

2. In this game user can roll the dice as many times he/she wants.

3. At a time whatever score he will get, it will be his current score.

4. When he will press hold button, That score will be added to the Main score.

5. But its not that simple. 2 conditions are here.

       1. when he rolls the dice, if he gets 1 then his current score will be zero and turn will be given to player 2.

      2. If you get 2 six in sequence then your main-score will be zero.

6. so you need to secure your current score and add it to the main-score.

7. Default target is 100 so when one of you will get the main score greater or equal to 100, he will win.

8. You can set target as you want from the input field.
